Portfolio

In recent years, my work has specifically focused on AI and programming, but prior to that, I spent more than 10 years writing copy for various tech websites. My portfolio spans over 10,000 published pieces, and it includes software and product reviews, curated lists and roundups, category hubs, news blurbs, and full-length features.

Muckrack - my official Muckrack profile which serves as a much better testament to my work in writing tech copy than this page does. Here you'll find a highlight reel of some of my favorite pieces, as well as what is by far the most complete compilation of my writing.

Medium - a more personal space for when I feel like I have something important to say that is too long for social media. It serves as the home for my piece on Long Covid, which, when you factor in the excerpt shares on sites like Twitter and Reddit, is easily the most read thing I've ever written.

AndroidPolice - the most recent and last stop on my tech content journey. I'm not sure how long my author profile and tag will remain active, but for now this links to all of my posts on the site which included lists, roundups, features, and a ton of commerce work. A lot of it was ephemeral, but there were also a few features and evergreen pieces that were some of my finest work.
